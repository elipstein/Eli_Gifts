# Interests & Gift Avenues

This file is the brief the weekly generator reads. It describes who the gifts are
for (me — Eli), the directions to mine each week, the budget bands to stay within,
and what to avoid. Edit it freely and often; the generator reads the whole file on
every run, so anything you add here shapes the next batch of ideas.

---

## Hard interests

The standing themes. Every idea should connect to at least one of these.

- **Espresso & coffee** — manual/lever espresso, hand grinders, dialing-in tools,
  scales, distribution/tamping gear, accessories.
- **3D printing** — Prusa ecosystem (MK4 / MK4S), upgrades and mods, quality
  filaments, enclosure/tooling add-ons, genuinely useful prints.
- **Israeli / Levantine & Mediterranean cooking** — specialty ingredients, spice
  blends, cookbooks, and the tools that make this cuisine easier.
- **Bread baking** — sourdough, banneton / lame / baking steel gear, home milling.
- **Pizza ovens** — peels and turning peels, stones/steels, IR thermometers, fuel,
  and other live-fire accessories.
- **Gardening (Mediterranean herbs)** — za'atar, thyme, oregano, sage, mint;
  growing, harvesting, and drying.
- **Home automation** — Home Assistant, Zigbee / Z-Wave / Matter devices, sensors,
  and tinker-friendly DIY kit.
- **Travel & points** — carry-on / EDC accessories, packing and cable organizers,
  airport/lounge gear.
- **Longevity & health** — sleep, recovery, measurement/tracking, and reputable
  supplements.

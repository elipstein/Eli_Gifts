# Gift Interests — input for the weekly generator

This file is the context the AI reads every run to discover gift ideas. Edit it freely:
add new **avenues to explore** as you think of them, prune categories that go stale, and
keep the **already own / avoid** list current so the same things don't keep coming back.

The recipient is an Israeli-American software engineer in the Seattle area, family of five.
Taste leans toward **well-made, design-forward, enthusiast-grade gear** over mass-market,
with a soft spot for anything with Israeli / Mediterranean cultural resonance and anything
that scratches a "life optimization / clever tool" itch. Prefer real, currently-purchasable
items with a working buy link. Prioritize unique, custom items (for example, an authoer signed book, rather than just the book).

- **3D printing** — owns a Prusa Mini. Upgrades, premium filaments, nozzles, enclosures, maker tools, well-designed printable kits. A higher-end printer is a valid splurge idea.
- **Israeli / Levantine & Mediterranean cooking** — central to the kitchen. Cookbooks (esp. Israeli/Levantine authors), specialty spices and pantry items, mortar & pestle and other prep tools, beautiful serveware.
- **Bread baking** — challah and sourdough. Bannetons, lames, Danish dough whisks, baking steel/stone, proofing boxes, grain mills, scoring tools, technique-driven baking books.
- **Pizza** — Neapolitan in an outdoor gas oven. Turning peels, launching peels, infrared thermometers, 00 flour sources, dough boxes, pizza-making accessories.
- **Pastry (serious hobbyist, French classics)** — silicone molds and entremet forms (Silikomart, de Buyer), tart rings, precision scales and thermometers, pastry books, and *pastry classes / experiences* (a standout splurge — short intensive programs especially).
- **Gardening / Mediterranean herbs** — drying racks, herb scissors, well-draining pots, grow lights, quality hand tools, Mediterranean-gardening books.
- **Home automation** — runs Home Assistant on a Raspberry Pi. HA-compatible sensors and devices (Aqara, ESPHome boards, Zigbee/Z-Wave gear), tinkering kits, clever automation hardware.
- **Travel & points** — packing systems, premium luggage, travel tech (chargers, trackers, noise-canceling headphones), comfort gear, family-of-five travel solutions.
- **Longevity / health optimization** — recovery and sleep gadgets, massage/mobility tools, well-designed quantified-self hardware. Keep it gadget/gear-focused.
